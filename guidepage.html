Introduction To The Game - Starting

Introduction Basics - Creating a Character

  1. You will be met with a customization screen where you pick and choose your style
  2. You will be granted a option to choose a class, later on you will be able to combine your class with another class.
  3. The classes are divided into groups A and B, and you can only mix classes from the opposite group, an example
    would be Warrior with Aegis or Tempest Mage with Thief. You unlock your second class at level 5 and they combine at level 8.

Introduction Basics - Combined Classes

Feel free to pick whatever class strikes your fancy, but BE CAREFUL, once you select your primary and secondary class this choice CANNOT
be undone. I strongly suggest that you all take a look at our discord before selecting your class, there we have explanatory guides and builds so
you can get a head start. As we have every guide for all combined classes at level 8.
